# Google Summer of Code 2026

Measurement Lab had to drop out of GSoC. The motivation for this decision is that, due to the massive usage of AI, we have seen a large volume of similar and incremental contributions that our team cannot handle.

We acknowledge and are thankful for those who put effort into contributing to our projects. We will review and process all the remaining contributions on our own schedule. We remain open for contributions outside of GSoC, so if you are willing to follow up on your initial ideas, feel free to reach out to us after the GSoC deadline.
